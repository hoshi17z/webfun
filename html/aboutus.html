Our Background

We are a small group of independent developers, but also a close circle of friends who are just happen to be in the same field.
We all had the same struggle with the complexity and redundant softwares that are available in today's world.
So, we decided to work together with each other and developed a new compact software which is now called the Starsilver Studio.



The Main Screen of our software

Our Software

The full name of our software is Starsilver Studio Art Program.
We have designed the software to cater to almost every professional from any digital background.
From thousand of painting, editing and correction tools, it provides fully packed services for users to create any kind of digital work.
We also combined the software with artificial inteligence which can exponentially improve user's workflow.
This is what makes our software different from the standards in the market.
